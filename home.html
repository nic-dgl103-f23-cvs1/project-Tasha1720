Consent - Mobile Public Video Program

A CURATED VIDEO PROGRAM OF THEMATICALLY ENGAGED ARTIST PROJECTS

The Consent video program emerged in association with Possibility Seeds national campaign for Consent Awareness. Consent is an on-going conversation — one that is empowered through intersectional discourse, vulnerability and respect for each other. The pieces displayed in this video program represent fragments of what it means to give, receive, acknowledge, and examine consent through various modes and mediums. The video program will continue to be updated throughout the convergent program as artists continue to engage with this important topic and participate in the continuing conversation.

Calling All Artists

The Comox Valley Art Gallery extends an open call for single channel videos in response to the theme of CONSENT. The video program will be displayed in The Comox Valley Art Gallery Window and the Plaza Media Gallery starting on November 18 and will run until January 13.

Practising visual and media artists, students, and collaborators are welcome to submit their finished video work or propose a concept for a new video work that can be completed and ready for presentation by November 14, 2023 or December 5, 2023. Click here to learn more.
